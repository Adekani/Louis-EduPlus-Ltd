The Founder's Journey

Louis Odubanjo, M.Ed., MCCT

At the heart of Louis EduPlus Ltd is the vision of Louis Odubanjo, an educational thinker, author, mentor, and advocate for transformative learning. Deeply passionate about restructuring the modern educational ecosystem, Louis has devoted his career to fostering pedagogy that moves beyond standardized "certificate mill" templates to engage the whole human person.

With rigorous academic training from the renowned University of Ibadan alongside ongoing affiliations as a Member of the Chartered College of Teaching (MCCT), Louis has spearheaded reform frameworks within both regional African boundaries and international contexts. His core thesis maintains that when schools focus solely on raw performance grades, they fail to develop critical innovators who operate with sound ethical guidance.

In addition to active consultancy, Louis continuously contributes to practice by mentoring the learning community of Lifeword Christian Academy. Through a synthesis of innovative pedagogy, parenting integration, and solid Christian tenets, Louis EduPlus Ltd aims to establish long-term transformation in how global institutions nurture future leadership.
